What is Science Runway?

The Science Runway is a project to attract young girls to careers in healthcare and life sciences by showcasing women who have successfully created careers in a variety of roles. The aim is to highlight the depth and breadth of various career trajectories, while providing a source of ongoing inspiration.

Find a scientist just like you.

Answer a few quick questions, and we'll help you find a mentor who shares your interests, quirks, and aspirations. All of our mentors were once girls who didn't know what the world held for them but had big dreams that they were willing to work hard to achieve.

Keep connected.

Sign up for our mailing list, and we'll send you updates on your mentor, new fun questions to answer, and let you be the first to know when we get new mentors on board.
